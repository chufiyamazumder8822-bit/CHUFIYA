About ASRLM & Jeevika Sakhi

The Assam State Rural Livelihood Mission (ASRLM) aims to reduce poverty and enhance livelihoods by promoting and supporting Self Help Groups (SHGs) across Assam.

As a Jeevika Sakhi, I act as a bridge between the government and rural communities, helping SHGs adopt digital tools, gain financial literacy, and improve their livelihoods.
